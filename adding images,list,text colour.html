    Looking for a hostel?Well you are in the right place.

    Hello! Welcome to my site. This site will help you find better place to stay while you are at Taita Taveta University. As you know the weather around TTU it's semi-arid,sometimes it's extremely hot during the day and it gets brass monkeys at night. When rains come it becomes really messy with the mud too. It rains cats and dogs. For this reason I decided to do a research to help you find a better stay.

    Getting a good house for your settlement as a student is a good idea, you dont want to stay in a place where you walk a thousand miles to fetch water or to get to a restaurant. You are a student and time is of great value to you,you don't want to waste it. For this reason I decided to create this site to help all students save time and also to guide the RE investors, that is to hint to you on areas you need to improve on to make a students life comfortable while staying in your respective houses

    Here's a list of hostels you'll have to consider next time you are looking for a place to stay while in TTU.

    The Baobab Hostel.

    Baobab Hostel is located at gate C. Baobab is a single floor building divided int two wings (GENTS WING AND LADIES WING) The BW is immediaetely on your left and the girls hostel avis. The rooms are divided into four classes namely:

    1. Superior rooms- Individual rooms.
    2. Basement rooms- Bedsitters rooms.
    3. Associates rooms- Two people room.

    Features that make Baobab an outstanding place to stay are as follows; cost friendly rents,rent is as low as KES 2500 for the Associates rooms and KES 3000 for Superior rooms and,KES 5000 for the Basement rooms. Other than low rent, Baobab is the only hostel offering free water to it's tenants three times a week that is on Mondays, Wednesdays and, Friday. Electricity is also taken care of with the management, you pay no electricity bill. Study tables and chairs are also fixed in all rooms,I stay in Baobab and here's a view of my room(Superior room)
